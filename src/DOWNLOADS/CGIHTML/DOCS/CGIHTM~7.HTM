5.1. Release notes

This is the fourth release of my cgihtml.a library. I am planning on enhancing this library, and I welcome any comments or suggestions. Please e-mail them to eekim@fas.harvard.edu.

This library is e-mail ware. Please send me e-mail if you use this library; I'd really like to hear your comments. Although I do not require it, I would appreciate attribution if you use my code.

5.2. Future releases

This library is nearing its final release. I hope to include cookie support, FastCGI support, and API support (for Apache, Netscape, and Microsoft servers). I may also attempt to port it to the Macintosh, and I want to improve generally portability. I am working on some other software that I hope will integrate well with cgihtml. Finally, I'd like to improve the robustness and fix all the bugs.

If you have any suggestions, I'd like to hear them. Feel free to e-mail me at eekim@hcs.harvard.edu.

5.3. Credits

Thanks to the countless people who have sent me suggestions and comments. You may contact me via e-mail at eekim@hcs.harvard.edu. My web page is located at http://hcs.harvard.edu/~eekim/.
